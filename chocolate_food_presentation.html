üç´ 5-Minute Chocolate Mug Cake üç´

Ingredients:
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips (optional)
Steps:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a microwave-safe mug
  2. Add milk and oil, stir well
  3. Add chocolate chips if desired
  4. Microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds
  5. Let cool for 1 minute and enjoy! üéâ
